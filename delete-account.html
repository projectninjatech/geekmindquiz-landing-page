Delete Account

If you would like to delete your GeekMind Quiz account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email from the email address registered in the app to: projectninjatech@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Account â€“ GeekMind Quiz.
  3. In the email body, include the following details (all of these can be found in the app under Settings):
    • Name (as registered in the app)
    • Email (the email used to sign in)
    • User ID (UID)
    • Reason for deletion (optional)

Verification & Processing Time

After we receive your email, our team will verify the details you provided (Name, Email, and UID) to confirm the request came from the account owner. Once verification is complete, your account and all associated data (name, email, quiz coins, scores, and leaderboard entries) will be permanently deleted within 48 hours.

Data Retention

We do not retain your personal data after deletion. Only anonymized usage statistics (which cannot identify you) may be kept for internal analytics or legal obligations.

If you reinstall the app or sign in again after deletion, a new profile will be created.

For further questions, please contact us at: projectninjatech@gmail.com.
