Privacy Policy

At GeekMind Quiz, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide when using our mobile application. By using GeekMind Quiz, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Information We Collect

When you use GeekMind Quiz, we may collect the following information:

  • Name & Email: Collected when you sign in using Google Sign-In (on Android) or Google/Apple Sign-In (on iOS). This helps us create and manage your account.
  • Gameplay Data: This includes your quiz coins, scores, and time taken to complete quizzes. We store this to show your progress and maintain accurate leaderboards.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Leaderboard & Progress: Your name, email, and gameplay data are used to display your achievements and ranking within the app.
  • Personalization: To enhance your experience and tailor quiz features to your profile.
  • Account Management: To allow you to securely log in, save progress, and access your account across devices.

Advertising

GeekMind Quiz uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device-related data (such as ad performance and engagement metrics) in order to serve personalized or non-personalized ads in compliance with Google’s policies. You can control your ad preferences through your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third-party companies for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with trusted service providers (such as Google and Apple sign-in services, and Google AdMob) strictly to enable app functionality.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. While we take every reasonable step to safeguard information, please note that no system is completely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To make such a request, please contact us at projectninjatech@gmail.com. We will process your request in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Children’s Privacy

GeekMind Quiz is not directed towards children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through the app or updated on this page. Continued use of GeekMind Quiz after changes indicates acceptance of the revised terms.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at: projectninjatech@gmail.com
