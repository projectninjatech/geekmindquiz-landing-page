Privacy Policy

At GeekMind Quiz, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using GeekMind Quiz, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you open the GeekMind Quiz app for the first time, we ask you to provide the following information:

Name: Your name is used to personalize your experience and to display on the leaderboard if you win the daily quiz.

Device ID: We collect and store your device's unique identifier (Device ID) to create a unique user profile in our database. This helps us manage your account and track your progress within the app.

How We Use Your Information

Personalization: We use your name to enhance your experience within the app, such as displaying it on the leaderboard when you achieve top scores.

User Identification: Your Device ID is used as a unique identifier to manage your account and ensure that your quiz results and coins are correctly attributed to you.

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your name and Device ID are stored securely in our database and are only used within the GeekMind Quiz app for the purposes outlined in this policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we have collected from you. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at projectninjatech@gmail.com. We will respond to your request as soon as possible.
